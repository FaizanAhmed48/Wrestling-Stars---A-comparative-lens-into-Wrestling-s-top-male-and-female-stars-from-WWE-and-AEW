The Rock

See more of The Rock on the WWE website.

The Rock's Biggest Matches

  • ROCK vs AUSTIN III

    • Long time rivals with 'Stone Cold' Steve Austin, these two certified legends put it all on the line for a final match in their historic trilogy spanning decades.

  • ROCK vs HOGAN

    • Two industry titans - one unforgettable match. Hulk Hogan of '80s stardom took the Hollywood star in the making to a challenging dream match audiences would not soon forget.

  • ROCK 'n SOCK CONNECTION vs EVOLUTION

    • The former unlikely yet charismatic duo of Mick Foley and The Rock pair up decades later against the dominant rising stars Batista (also of Hollywood fame) and Randy Orton in Evolution, alongside wrestling legend Ric Flair.
